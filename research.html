Econometrics Research

Crime Rates and Economic Development: A Cross-Country Analysis

2024

Innovative econometric research analyzing correlation between crime rates and GDP across 20 countries, employing novel methodology with crime rate as dependent variable, achieving R² of 0.72 correlation coefficient.

Methodological Innovation

Took a novel approach going beyond the norm by considering crime rate as the dependent variable and GDP as the independent variable, contrary to what is normally done in other research studies.
