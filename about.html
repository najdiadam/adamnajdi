Who I Am

I'm Adam Najdi, a 21-year-old senior Economics student at the American University of Beirut, deeply passionate about developmental economics and sustainable growth solutions. My journey combines academic excellence with real-world impact, from co-founding economic research initiatives to supporting communities during challenging times.

My Passion

I'm particularly interested in developmental economics and how data-driven approaches can create meaningful change in communities. My work with the Global Strategy Review (GSR) initiative reflects this passion, where we're developing frameworks for sustainable, circular economic growth in Lebanon, focusing on Agriculture, Tourism, Industry, and Services.
